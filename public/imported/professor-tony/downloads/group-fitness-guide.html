Detailed Certification Information

ACE Group Fitness Instructor (ACE-GFI)

Cost: $399-699 depending on package

Prerequisites: 18+, CPR/AED certification

Exam: 150 questions, 3 hours

Recertification: Every 2 years, 2.0 CEUs required

Curriculum Highlights:

  • Exercise science fundamentals
  • Class design and programming
  • Music and cueing techniques
  • Participant safety and modifications
  • Behavior change coaching
AFAA Group Fitness Instructor

Cost: $299-599 depending on format

Prerequisites: 18+, CPR/AED certification

Format: Online self-study or 2-day live workshop

Recertification: Every 2 years, 15 CEUs required

Curriculum Highlights:

  • 5 Questions approach to class design
  • Basic exercise science
  • Class formats (step, cardio, strength, flexibility)
  • Cueing and communication
  • Safety and emergency procedures

Les Mills Certifications

Les Mills offers certifications in specific pre-choreographed programs used worldwide:

  • BODYPUMP - Barbell strength training
  • BODYCOMBAT - Martial arts-inspired cardio
  • BODYATTACK - High-energy cardio
  • RPM - Indoor cycling
  • BODYSTEP - Step-based cardio
  • BODYBALANCE/BODYFLOW - Yoga/Pilates/Tai Chi fusion
Pro Tip: Many gyms prefer instructors with Les Mills certifications because they can teach branded classes. However, getting a general GFI certification first gives you more flexibility and foundational knowledge.

Getting Started: Recommended Path

  1. Get certified in CPR/AED (required for all certifications)
  2. Obtain a primary GFI certification (ACE or AFAA recommended)
  3. Gain teaching experience through volunteering or subbing
  4. Add specialty certifications based on your interests and job market demand
  5. Continue education to stay current and expand your skills

Job Opportunities

  • Commercial gyms (LA Fitness, 24 Hour, Equinox)
  • Boutique studios (SoulCycle, OrangeTheory, F45)
  • Community recreation centers
  • Corporate wellness programs
  • Senior living facilities
  • Virtual/online fitness platforms
Salary Range: Group fitness instructors typically earn $15-50 per class depending on location, facility type, and experience. Full-time positions with benefits are available at some facilities, especially in fitness director roles.
